Our System

The SkinSense - Skin Disease Detection system utilizes state-of-the-art Deep Learning models to analyze skin lesions with high precision. By processing images uploaded by users, our system can identify a wide range of conditions—from Acne to Melanoma—providing instant, preliminary insights into skin health to encourage timely medical consultation.

Our Mission

The mission of SkinSense is to bridge the gap between advanced technology and healthcare accessibility. We aim to empower individuals by providing a reliable tool for early detection, which is critical in successfully treating serious skin conditions.

  • Accessible early screening for all users.
  • Reducing the time between symptom onset and consultation.
  • Supporting dermatologists with AI-driven insights.
